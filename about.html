About Me

My name is Coire Walker and I do real estate photography in the San Diego area. I began shooting real estate as an income supplement to my music teaching, but I really ended up enjoying the work! My goal as a photographer is to showcase the architecture, landscape, and design elements that are unique to each property I shoot. Typically I deliver completed photos within 48hrs.

Please feel free to contact me for current rates and availability.
