About Me

My name is Coire Walker and I do real estate photography in the Denver area. I began shooting real estate as an income supplement to my music teaching, but I ended up really enjoying the work! My goal as a photographer is to showcase the architecture, landscape, and design elements that are unique to each property I shoot. All images use natural lighting and are shot in HDR for maximal post-processing flexibility.

Each and every image is edited individually. Although this takes significantly more time than batch processing or outsourcing, it allows me to create the best possible images for my clients.

Shoots cost $175 and will provide 20-25 high-quality images. Photos are delivered within 48hrs of a shoot. If you require more images, just let me know.
